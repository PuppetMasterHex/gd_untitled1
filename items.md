# Weapons
Every type of weapon has its own experience level.  
On weapon levelup two [Attributes](attributes.md) of the Player are raised depending on the type of the weapon.

## Small Weapons

### Daggers
* Agility +1
* Speed +2

### Throwing weapons
* Agility +1
* Speed +2

### Unarmed
* SP +1
* Mind +1

## Medium Weapons
### Swords
* Strength +1
* Mind +2

### Maces
* MP +1
* Body +2

### Whips
* Mind +1
* SP +2

## Large Weapons
### Spears
* Agility +1
* Strength +2

### Axes
* Body +2
* Strength +1

### Bows
* Agility +1
* SP +1
