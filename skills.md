# Skills

Skills are combat techniques that can be executed at the expense of SP.  
Some Skills may also combine a weapon with magic to form a magical attack.  
Most Skills can be used with different weapon type, e.g. a skill may require [Whips](items.md#whips) at level 18 and [Fire](magic.md#fire) at level 14 to execute [Fire Whip](#fire-whip).  
Effects of skill only persist from the begin of the turn until the skill was used (in case of Speed up%) or while the skill is executed (others).  
[Conditions](conditions.md) applied by the skills may remain for a longer duration.
